01
Cipher Tool
Paste any encoded text and it will try to decode it automatically; Caesar, Base64, Morse, Atbash, Rail Fence, and more.
02
Frequency Analysis
Shows how often each letter appears in your text. Useful for figuring out simple substitution ciphers.
03
Vigenère Cracker
Tries to crack a Vigenère cipher by testing words as possible keys, or by brute-forcing short key combinations.
04
Beaufort Cracker
Tries to crack a Beaufort cipher by testing dictionary words as the key.
05
Hexahue Decoder
Decodes Hexahue; A cipher where each letter is represented by a pattern of six colors. Enter the color codes and get the text.
06
IoC Analyzer
Helps you figure out the key length of a cipher. Shows a chart; Taller bars mean a more likely key length.
07
Word Finder
Know some letters but not all? Enter the possible letters for each position and it will find matching words.
08
Keyboard Heatmap
Highlights which keys are used most in your text, shown on a keyboard layout. Supports QWERTY, AZERTY, QWERTZ, and Dvorak.
09
Punycode
Converts Punycode (used in domain names) to readable text and back.
10
Anagram Solver
Rearranges scrambled letters into valid words. Supports pattern-based solving to narrow down the results.
11
Base64 Bruteforce
Given a Base64 string with unknown casing, tries all case combinations per chunk and shows every valid printable decoding.
12
Spaceless Binary
Recovers text from binary, decimal, octal, or hex that has had its spacing and leading zeros removed. Supports all traversal modes plus interactive byte-by-byte selection.
